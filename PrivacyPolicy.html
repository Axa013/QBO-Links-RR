Privacy Policy (Internal Use Only)

Last updated: 08/07/2025

1 · Purpose

This internal integration connects QuickBooks Online (QBO) with monday.com to support accounting and operations at Callahan Autoparts. It synchronizes accounting records such as Sales Receipts and Refund Receipts, and may be extended to other QBO objects (e.g., Invoices, Bills) strictly for internal workflows.

2 · Data processed

Limited accounting data required for reconciliation and filing, including:

  • Document identifiers (e.g., Id, DocNumber, TxnDate, totals, taxes).
  • Line items (item names, quantities, amounts, tax lines).
  • Related references and metadata (e.g., CustomerRef name, DepositTo account name, create/update timestamps).
  • PDF copies of supported documents (when needed for internal record-keeping).

Not processed: bank credentials, payment card numbers, or payroll PII beyond what appears on the accounting documents themselves.

3 · Sharing

Data is not shared outside Callahan Autoparts. monday.com acts as a data processor under our corporate subscription. No data is sold or used for advertising.

4 · Retention

Records are retained for up to 7 years for audit/compliance, then deleted per policy. Earlier deletion may be performed upon a valid internal request at josue.callahan@gmail.com.

5 · Security

  • Transport security via HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.
  • OAuth 2.0 with encrypted credential storage in n8n.
  • Access restricted to authorized employees (least privilege, MFA/SSO where available).
  • Data stored inside monday.com’s encrypted infrastructure and our secured n8n environment.

6 · Access & Revocation

Access can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the app, which triggers token revocation with Intuit. Internal admins can also request removal of synchronized records from monday.com.

7 · Changes

We may update this policy when integrations expand to new QBO objects. Material changes will update the “Last updated” date above.

Contact: josue.callahan@gmail.com
