Privacy Policy (Internal Use Only)

Last updated:08/07/2025

1 · Purpose

This integration synchronises refund-receipt data from QuickBooks Online to an internal monday.com board used by the finance team of Callahan Autoparts.

2 · Data processed

Receipt IDs, dates, customer names, line items, amounts and taxes.

3 · Sharing

Data is not shared outside Callahan Autoparts. monday.com acts as a data processor under our corporate subscription.

4 · Retention

Records are kept for 7 years for audit purposes and may be deleted sooner on request via josue.callahan@gmail.com.

5 · Security

Data travels over TLS 1.2+ and is stored within monday.com’s encrypted infrastructure, protected by SSO and MFA.
